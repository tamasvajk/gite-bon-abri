About us

Located on the Tour du Mont Blanc, our gite can accommodate up to 57 people in summer and 50 in winter. Our gite has one double bed room, 5 rooms with 6 beds and a large dormitory with 18 beds. Our beds have fitted sheets, Nordic duvets and pillows. The bathrooms are spacious and modern (unlimited hot water). Our healthy cuisine prioritises regional and seasonal products. A spacious communal area has room for 50 people for eating meals and enjoying entertainment.
