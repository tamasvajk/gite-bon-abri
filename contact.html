We are located in the Vallon de Champex, 1.2 km from Champex - Lac, on walking and hiking routes (Val d'Arpette, Bovine, Ferret). In Winter, the cross country skiing trail passes by our windows and the Breya ski lift is just 600 metres away. The chalet is accessible on skis. The Bon Abri is accessible all year round in well equipped private vehicles.
